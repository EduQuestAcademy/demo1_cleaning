Frequently Asked Questions

Everything you want to know about
cleaning services in Victoria BC

How much does house cleaning cost in Victoria BC? +

House cleaning in Victoria BC typically ranges from $120–$420 depending on home size and service type. A 2-bedroom regular clean starts around $120–$160, while a full deep clean for a 3-bedroom home is $220–$320. Move-out cleans run $250–$420. All quotes are free and no-obligation.

Are you insured and bonded in British Columbia? +

Yes — Marie's Cleaning Services is fully insured, bonded, and all staff are covered under WorkSafeBC (WCB). You are completely protected on every visit. We also carry general liability insurance for any accidental damage.

Do you bring your own cleaning supplies? +

Yes, we bring all professional-grade cleaning supplies and equipment. We also offer certified eco-friendly, non-toxic products at no extra charge — just let us know when booking.

Do you offer Airbnb cleaning and same-day turnovers? +

Absolutely. We specialize in Airbnb and VRBO turnover cleaning across Greater Victoria, including same-day turnovers. We coordinate with your booking calendar and send photo confirmation after every clean.

What's included in a move-out clean in Victoria? +

Our move-out cleaning includes a full deep clean — inside all appliances, cabinets and drawers, baseboards, light switches, door frames, bathroom sanitization, and all floors. We follow BC rental property standards to maximize your damage deposit return.

Do you offer commercial cleaning contracts in Victoria? +

Yes. We offer flexible commercial contracts for offices, retail stores, restaurants, medical clinics, strata buildings, and more. Contracts can be daily, weekly, or monthly with after-hours or weekend scheduling. No long-term lock-ins required.

What areas of Victoria do you serve? +

We serve all of Greater Victoria including Downtown Victoria, Oak Bay, Saanich, James Bay, Fairfield, Fernwood, Esquimalt, Langford, Colwood, View Royal, Sidney, Saanichton, Central Saanich, North Saanich, Sooke, and Gordon Head.

What is your satisfaction guarantee? +

If you're not 100% satisfied with any part of your clean, contact us within 24 hours and we'll return to re-clean the area at absolutely no charge. We stand behind every visit — no questions asked.
